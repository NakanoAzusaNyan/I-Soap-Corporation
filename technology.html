Our Technology

Innovative, eco-friendly solutions to ensure effective cleaning.

Eco-friendly Saponification

A safe and efficient process that transforms natural oils into soap without harmful residues. This ensures both safety for the user and minimal environmental impact.

Innovative Formulation

By combining natural oils, glycerin, and essential oils, we deliver products that are effective for cleaning while being gentle on skin and environmentally conscious.

Safe pH Balancing System

Our technology maintains a skin-friendly pH level to prevent irritation, making I SOAP suitable for sensitive skin and everyday use.

Eco-friendly Packaging

We use biodegradable materials for our packaging to reduce waste and support sustainability, ensuring our commitment to the environment.
